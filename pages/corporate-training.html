How It Works

Four straightforward steps from first conversation to program delivery.

Schedule Your Discovery Call
1

Discovery Call (15-20 Minutes)

What happens:

We get on the phone to understand what you need. What challenges is your team facing? What skills do they need to develop? How many people are we training?

What we'll ask:
  • What specific topics or compliance issues are top of mind?
  • Who's attending (job titles, experience levels)?
  • Any company-specific situations we should address?
  • Preferred delivery format: on-site or virtual?
What you'll learn:

Whether corporate training makes sense for your situation, or if sending individuals to our public seminars is a better fit.

2

Custom Proposal (Within 48 Hours)

What you'll receive:
  • Recommended program(s) based on your needs
  • Detailed agenda and learning outcomes
  • Delivery format options (on-site or live virtual)
  • Transparent pricing
  • Sample materials
What happens next:

Review the proposal. We adjust based on your feedback. No pressure, no sales tactics—just a straightforward recommendation based on what we heard in the discovery call.

3

Content Customization (Collaborative Process)

This is where we make it yours:

  • We incorporate your policies and procedures into the training
  • Add your real scenarios and case studies
  • Include your company's specific compliance concerns
  • Customize examples to your industry
  • Review draft materials with you before delivery
Timeline:

Typically 2-4 weeks from approval to delivery, depending on customization depth. Need it faster? We can usually accommodate rush requests.

4

Delivery & Follow-Up

Two delivery options—your choice:

On-Site Delivery

We come to your office (anywhere in the U.S.). Same interactive format as our public seminars. Materials provided for all participants. Maximum team interaction and networking.

Best for larger groups (20+ participants)
Live Virtual Delivery

Interactive online training via Zoom or Teams. Same expert instructors, same practical content. Digital materials and resources included. Breakout rooms, polls, live Q&A—fully interactive.

Best for distributed teams or 10-30 participants
Both formats include:
  • Experienced instructors (no junior trainers)
  • Interactive exercises and real-world scenarios
  • Comprehensive participant materials
  • Post-training consultation (30 days)
  • Certificate of completion for all participants
After the program:

We follow up to answer any lingering questions and provide additional resources. You'll have access to us for 30 days post-training if issues come up.
