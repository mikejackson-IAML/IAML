A Message from Our President

Building Careers, Not Just Compliance

For over four decades, I've watched HR professionals transform their careers after training with us. Not because we teach them rules to memorize, but because we show them how employment law actually works in practice.

Our instructors aren't academics who read about cases. They're practicing attorneys who argue these cases, negotiate these settlements, and advise companies through these exact situations every single day.

When you leave an IAML program, you don't just have new knowledge. You have a new perspective—the same perspective that the attorneys your company relies on bring to every decision. That perspective changes everything about how you approach your work.

I founded IAML on a simple belief: HR professionals deserve the same quality of legal education that attorneys receive. Our track record of 15,000+ alumni from companies across every industry proves this approach works.

Whether you're looking to advance your career, protect your organization, or simply become more confident in your daily decisions—we're here to help you get there.

Warmly,
