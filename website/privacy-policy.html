Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 31, 2025

The Institute for Applied Management & Law, Inc. ("IAML", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you use our website or services.

1. Who We Are

IAML provides training programs and publishes professional content for businesses and their employees. This Privacy Policy applies to all data collected through our website and related services.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information from users:

  • Name and contact details (e.g., email address, phone number)
  • Information submitted via forms (e.g., registrations, service inquiries)
  • Information from cookies and similar technologies (see Section 5)

We do not collect or store:

  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Payment information (Stripe handles all payment processing)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our services
  • To communicate with you (e.g., registration confirmation, customer support)
  • To improve our website and content using analytics tools
  • To fulfill any legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

As per applicable data protection laws, we process your data based on:

  • Consent (e.g., accepting cookies, form submissions)
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., when registering for our services)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving services via analytics)

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Ensure proper website functionality
  • Understand how users interact with our site via:
    • Google Analytics
    • Hotjar

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings.

6. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. We only share data with trusted service providers that help us operate our services, including:

  • Stripe (payment processor)
  • Google Analytics and Hotjar (analytics providers)

These providers are contractually obligated to safeguard your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services, or until you request its deletion.

8. Your Rights

Even though we do not intentionally target users in the EU, we strive to follow fair privacy practices. You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@iaml.com.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure hosting environments
  • Access control and authentication protocols
  • Regular monitoring and updates

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the revision date clearly posted.

11. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact:

  • info@iaml.com
  • Institute for Applied Management & Law, Inc.
